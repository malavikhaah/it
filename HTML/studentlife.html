Placements

Career Guidance and Placement Cell facilitates and inspires students for achieving their employment, higher education and entrepreneurial aspiration. The cell supports the students in terms of placement activities, and helps students through training and career awareness programmes to make them ready for employment. With active student feedback and involvement CGPC is trying to constantly upgrade students’ skill and thereby improve their chance of getting placed.

Our alumni are visible in reputed companies, and are taking up higher studies in India and abroad. The Cell shall support the recruiter’s requirement of finding employable and valuable candidates.

GEC Idukki students are absorbed in reputed companies such as Infosys, TCS, CTS, UST, IBS, HCL, Wipro, Quest, Qburst, Mitsogo, Torry Harris, Envestnet, Analytics Quotient, Talrop, Alogomox, 6D Technologies, Technimark, Vantenburg, Caramond Electronics, Tata Elxsi, VVDN, Gadegon, Larsen & Toubro, Visteon, Elecrtex, Schneider electric, Bosch, TVS, MRF, Ashok Leyland, Arnia, Walkaroo, TVS, CryptoMility, Baker Hughes, Naman, Ensoft, Rane. CGPC values its partnership with recruiters and Alumni, and remains committed to making the recruiting experience productive and positive.

CGPC is working on the Inspire-Aspire pathway on students’ professional development. We are changing and making progress. GEC Idukki is celebrating its Silver Jubilee. We welcome all recruiters this year to start a new journey, forge new partnerships and experience GEC Idukki.

IT Department Library

The IT Department Library of GEC Idukki is a dedicated knowledge hub designed to support students and faculty of the Information Technology branch. It provides specialized resources in the field of Computer Science and IT, including textbooks, reference books, project reports, and e–resources.

The library is equipped with modern facilities and gives access to e–journals, research papers, magazines, and digital learning platforms. It serves as a quiet and resourceful space for students to study, research, and collaborate on academic projects.

Collection Highlights: The IT Department Library has a collection of core IT and Computer Science books, access to online learning portals, coding resources, and digital repositories to enhance technical knowledge and research skills.
